  • Laws & Regulations
    • Current Program Statutes
      Statutory authorization for the Office of Statewide Health Planning and Development and its programs is primarily found in Division 107 of the Health and Safety Code, §127000 et seq.
    • Current Program Regulations
      Most OSHPD program regulations are found in Division 7 of Title 22 of the California Code of Regulations (CCR), commencing with §90001. Building standards are found in Title 24.
